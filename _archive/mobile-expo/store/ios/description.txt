SilentSuite — Encrypted Calendar, Contacts & Tasks

Your personal information deserves privacy. SilentSuite provides end-to-end encrypted sync for your calendar events, contacts, and tasks across all your devices.

HOW IT WORKS
• Sign in with your SilentSuite account
• Your data is encrypted on your device before syncing
• Only you can decrypt your information — not even we can read it
• Choose Bridge Mode to sync with your phone's native Calendar and Contacts apps
• Or use Walled Garden mode for maximum privacy

FEATURES
• Calendar: Monthly view, event creation and editing, all-day events
• Contacts: Searchable address book with phone, email, and address fields
• Tasks: Priority levels, due dates, completion tracking, sorting
• Bridge Mode: Seamlessly integrates with iOS Calendar and Contacts
• Background Sync: Automatic updates every 15 minutes
• Dark Theme: Easy on the eyes, beautiful navy and emerald design
• Offline Access: Your data is always available, even without internet

PRIVACY FIRST
SilentSuite uses the Etebase protocol for zero-knowledge encryption. Your encryption keys never leave your device. We cannot read your data, and neither can anyone else.

OPEN SOURCE
SilentSuite is built on open-source technology. Verify our security claims yourself at silentsuite.io.
