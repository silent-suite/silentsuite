SilentSuite — Encrypted Calendar, Contacts & Tasks

Your personal information deserves privacy. SilentSuite provides end-to-end encrypted sync for your calendar events, contacts, and tasks across all your devices.

HOW IT WORKS
• Sign in with your SilentSuite account
• Your data is encrypted on your device before syncing
• Only you can decrypt your information — not even we can read it
• Choose Bridge Mode to sync with your phone's native calendar, contacts, and task apps
• Or use Walled Garden mode for maximum privacy

FEATURES
• Calendar: Monthly view with event dots, day agenda, full CRUD
• Contacts: Alphabetical list with search, detail view, tap-to-call
• Tasks: Priority levels, due dates, sort by date/priority/name
• Bridge Mode: Works with your existing calendar, contacts, and task apps (OpenTasks/Tasks.org)
• Background Sync: Automatic updates via Android sync adapter
• Dark Theme: Navy and emerald design
• Offline Access: Data always available without internet

PRIVACY FIRST
SilentSuite uses the Etebase protocol for zero-knowledge encryption. Your encryption keys never leave your device. We cannot read your data, and neither can anyone else.

OPEN SOURCE
SilentSuite is built on open-source technology. Verify our security claims at silentsuite.io.
