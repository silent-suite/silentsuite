SilentSuite provides end-to-end encrypted sync for your calendar events, contacts, and tasks using the Etebase protocol.

Your data is encrypted on your device before syncing. Only you hold the encryption keys.

Features:
* Calendar with monthly view and full CRUD
* Contacts with search and detail views
* Tasks with priorities, due dates, and completion tracking
* Bridge Mode: sync to your phone's native apps
* Walled Garden mode: data stays encrypted in-app
* Background sync
* Dark theme
* Offline access

Requires a SilentSuite account (app.silentsuite.io) or self-hosted Etebase server.

Source code: https://github.com/silent-suite/silentsuite-app
